CARBONARA

DESCRIPTION

Recipe for Carbonara with cream and bacon: A different and easy version of Carbonara with the beloved cream will offer rich flavor to your table in just 25 minutes. These bacon pasta dishes are sure to become a favorite dish in every home thanks to their simplicity and the deliciousness they can offer at any moment! You can accompany your Carbonara and enjoy it with a white wine.

INGREDIENTS

  • 500 grams Spaghetti No. 6 MISKO
  • 120 grams smoked bacon, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 300 ml light cream
  • 150 grams grated Parmesan or Kefalograviera cheese
  • 80-100 ml water in which the pasta boiled
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground pepper

STEPS

  1. To prepare the pasta, fill a large pot with 5 liters of water and bring it to a high heat.
  2. Meanwhile, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a shallow pan and sauté the bacon for 2-3 minutes until it starts to brown.
  3. Transfer the bacon to a plate, add another 2 tablespoons of olive oil to the pan, and sauté the onion over medium heat for 4-5 minutes.
  4. Once the water in the large pot starts boiling, add 35 grams of salt and add the Spaghetti No. 6 MISKO.
  5. Stir with a fork and let them boil for the time indicated on the package.
  6. When the onion has softened, add the minced garlic, sauté for 1 minute, and pour in the light cream, bacon, and freshly ground pepper.
  7. Reduce the heat and let all the ingredients simmer for 4-5 minutes.
  8. Towards the end, add half of the grated cheese, stirring it in.
  9. Using quick motions, transfer the cooked pasta directly from the pot to the sauce and gently mix to evenly coat the ingredients.
  10. If the carbonara sauce is too thick, add some of the water in which the pasta boiled and continue stirring.
  11. Serve the Carbonara immediately, sprinkling it with the remaining grated cheese.
